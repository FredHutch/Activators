The datasets useful for TAD prediction benchmarking:
./yeast213/ 
212 TF from Ariel (additionally, the folder contains some cytoplamatic and mitochondrial datasets; for details see ./yeast213/README.txt

./titz/ 
371 proteins from Titz et. al 2006 (PMID: 16464826)

./sequences17
17 sequences with konwn TADs (e.g Gal4, Hap4, Ino2)

./Stark2018/
53 TF including a fragment that's recovered as the tAD. (PMID 30006452)
